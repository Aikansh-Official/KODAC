Endomorphs

Endomorphs typically have a higher body fat percentage, larger bone structure, and slower metabolism. Training should focus on higher intensity cardio and full-body resistance training.

Mesomorphs

Mesomorphs

Mesomorphs have a naturally athletic physique with well-defined muscles and a responsive metabolism. They respond well to both strength training and moderate cardio.

Ectomorphs

Ectomorphs

Ectomorphs have a naturally lean build with difficulty gaining weight. Training should focus on heavy compound lifts with lower rep ranges and limited cardio to maximize muscle growth.
